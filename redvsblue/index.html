Start Screen


The whole point of this game is to get the other player's health down. The above shows the critical health of the player or bot. When either player gets hit, their health drains. The image above shows the health bar for both players.



Enemy attack


When you're playing the single player mode, you are going up against a bot. When you go up against the bot, you must know that he attacks by sticking on you and draining your health. It's a big part of the game because it adds awareness when going against him. When he attaches on the player, he or she must attack quickly, punch or kick to get him off you. When you beat or kick him, he will teleport o a new location and come at you again. Stay aware and punch him on time to get him away from you. Rinse and repeat, and you shall be sufficient.

These are all the game over screens in my games. These both are used in multiplayer and single-player and are determined by who wins the match. When met with this screen, you must instead press retry or homesite. If you press retry, you will play the game again, this being single-player or multiplayer. If you press homesite, it will take you back to the main menu(pic above in start screen section).
